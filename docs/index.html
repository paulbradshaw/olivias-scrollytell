Let's look at the smoking habits for those who smoke in 2022

According to the data, The North has the highest number of smokers with 24%.

Females across the UK have a higher number of smokers compared to men

Across the UK, those who are single have the highest number of smokers, followed by those who are married.

Let's explore the smoking habits of The North.

In the North, the most popular form of smoking are from packets, which can cost anywhere from £10 to £16.

Among those who smoke in the North, those on a gross income of £5,200 to £10,400 have the highest number of smokers.

The data shows that more cigarettes are smoked on the weekend opposed to those on the weekend, with one participant claiming to have smoked 40 on a weekend and 15 in the week.
